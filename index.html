Why Propane?

Propane is clean, efficient, and reliable. None of that charcoal mess, none of that gasoline stink. Just good, honest fuel that’ll keep your grill going and your home warm.

Benefits of Propane

  • Burns cleaner than charcoal or wood.
  • Heats evenly and quickly.
  • Safe when handled properly.
  • Perfect for grilling steaks, burgers, and hot dogs — taste the meat, not the heat!

Propane Accessories

Every man should have the right accessories: tanks, regulators, hoses, and a grill that respects the propane lifestyle. I sell propane and propane accessories, and I take pride in that fact.

Safety Tips

Always check your connections. Don’t smoke near the tank. Treat propane with the respect it deserves. Do that, and you’ll be grilling with confidence for years to come.
